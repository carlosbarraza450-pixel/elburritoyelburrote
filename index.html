The Legacy

At El Burrito y el Burrote, we don’t just serve food; we serve a legacy. Founded and operated by Yamilex Rojas and Maria Rojas, our kitchen is the heart of a proud, women-owned business built on a foundation of "blood, sweat, and tears."

Originating from the vibrant streets of Ciudad Juárez, Yamilex and Maria moved to Denver with a singular mission: to share the soul-deep seasoning and time-honored traditions of their home.

Every plate we serve is a testament to our journey and a true, unadulterated taste of Ciudad Juárez.
