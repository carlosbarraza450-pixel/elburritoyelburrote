Our Story

At El Burrito y el Burrote, we don’t just serve food; we serve a legacy. Founded and operated by Yamilex Rojas and Maria Rojas, our kitchen is the heart of a proud, women-owned business.


Originating from the vibrant streets of Ciudad Juárez, Yamilex and Maria moved to Denver to share the soul-deep seasoning and time-honored traditions of their home.
