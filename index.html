Our Story

At El Burrito y el Burrote, we don’t just serve food; we serve a legacy. Founded and operated by Yamilex Rojas and Maria Rojas, our kitchen is the heart of a proud, women-owned business built on a foundation of "blood, sweat, and tears."

Originating from the vibrant streets of Ciudad Juárez, we moved to Denver to share the soul-deep seasoning and time-honored traditions of our home. Every plate we serve is a testament to our journey and a true taste of Mexico.
