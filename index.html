Our Story

At El Burrito y el Burrote, we don’t just serve food; we serve a legacy. Founded and operated by Yamilex Rojas and Maria Rojas, our kitchen is the heart of a proud, women-owned business built on a foundation of "blood, sweat, and tears." Originating from the vibrant streets of Ciudad Juárez, Yamilex and Maria moved to Denver with a singular mission: to share the soul-deep seasoning and time-honored traditions of their home.

Cooking is more than a profession for us—it is a lifelong passion. While many claim authenticity, we live it. Our recipes have been whispered down through generations, preserved with care, and brought to life with the same fire and flavor you’d find in the heart of Mexico. Every plate we serve is a testament to our journey and a true, unadulterated taste of Ciudad Juárez.
